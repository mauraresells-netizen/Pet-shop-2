Our Story

We started Pet Shop right here in Glasgow with one simple idea — every pet deserves quality care, and every owner deserves a shop they can actually trust. No gimmicks, no overpriced fads.

Whether you have a Staffie, a house cat, or a family of guinea pigs — we have everything under one roof at prices that make sense.

Every product is hand-picked by our team. If we would not use it ourselves, it does not make the shelves. That is our only rule.
